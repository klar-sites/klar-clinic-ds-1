Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

No referral is needed — you can book a consultation directly. If you have imaging or notes from another clinician, bring them along and we'll review everything together at your first visit.
The large majority of shoulder problems resolve with structured rehabilitation. Surgery is only ever recommended when it is genuinely the best option for you — and we'll explain exactly why.
Most patients feel meaningful improvement within six weeks, with full recovery typically between three and six months, depending on the condition and your goals.
We hold appointments open each week for new patients and can usually offer a consultation within a few days. For urgent injuries, call us and we'll do our best to see you sooner.
