Urgent care

Injured outside opening hours?

For acute shoulder injuries that can't wait for an appointment, our urgent-care line is staffed by a clinician every day until 22:00. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, severe chest pain, or loss of sensation, please call 112 immediately.

Getting here

Parking & public transport

We're easy to reach by car, bus or metro — right on Riverside Walk in central Stockholm.

By car

Step-free underground parking is available directly beneath the building, with the first two hours free for patients — just bring your ticket to reception to validate it. Two accessible bays are reserved next to the lift.

By public transport

We're a four-minute walk from Riverside metro station (blue and green lines), and bus routes 53, 76 and 96 stop at Riverside Walk right outside our door. Cycle racks are available at the main entrance.
