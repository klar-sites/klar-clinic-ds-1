About Klar Clinic

Calm, expert care — built entirely around you

We are a specialist shoulder, sports-injury and rehabilitation clinic. For two decades we have helped people move freely again — pairing orthopaedic expertise with genuinely personal care, in a setting designed to feel anything but clinical.

Our story

Founded on a simple belief

Klar Clinic began in 2006, when orthopaedic surgeon Dr. Elin Sandström grew frustrated by a system that treated scans rather than people. Too many patients were rushed toward surgery, handed a leaflet, and left to recover alone. She believed shoulder care could be calmer, clearer, and far more human.

So she built a clinic around one idea: that lasting recovery comes from understanding the whole person — their pain, their goals, and the life they want to get back to. From a single consultation room in central Stockholm, Klar has grown into a multidisciplinary team of surgeons, physicians and physiotherapists who work side by side under one roof.

What makes us different is continuity. One named specialist follows your whole journey, from first assessment to final session, with non-surgical options always explored first. The result is care that feels considered, unhurried, and entirely yours.

Our philosophy

Movement is medicine — when it is guided with care

Our mission is to help every patient return to the movement they love, with confidence and without fear of pain. We treat people, not just injuries, and we measure success not by procedures performed but by lives returned to motion.

“The goal is never just less pain — it is a shoulder you trust again, and a recovery you understand every step of the way.”
What we stand for

The values behind every plan

Four principles shape how we listen, diagnose and treat — and they hold true from your very first visit to your last.

Compassion

We listen first. Every visit is unhurried, and every question is welcome — because feeling heard is part of healing.

Precision

Accurate diagnosis is everything. On-site imaging and hands-on testing pinpoint the true source of pain before we treat.

Partnership

Recovery is a team effort. We explain every option clearly and make each decision together, with you firmly in the lead.

Progress

Clear milestones keep recovery moving. We track each step so you always know how far you have come and what comes next.

Our approach

Care shaped around your life

No two shoulders — and no two lives — are the same. We build every plan around your goals, your routine, and the activities you want to return to.

  • A thorough, unhurried first consultation to understand your history and goals
  • One named specialist who guides your whole journey from start to finish
  • Non-surgical options explored first, with surgery recommended only when truly best
  • Personalised rehabilitation plans, reviewed and adjusted at every visit
  • A home exercise programme that fits around work, sport and family life
  • Clear milestones so you always understand the path ahead
